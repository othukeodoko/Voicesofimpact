Common Questions

Is VOI really free?

+

Yes! VOI is Kingdom work, not a business. There are no hidden fees, upsells, or costs. We believe multiplication should be accessible to everyone serious about discipleship.

What's the time commitment?

+

8 Monday nights (7:00-9:00 PM via WhatsApp)
Daily PCS practice (15-30 minutes)
Weekly discipleship (starting Week 6, 1 hour/week)
Total: ~2-3 hours/week. Less than binge-watching one show.

What if I'm not a "leader type"?

+

Perfect! VOI isn't about personality‚Äîit's about faithfulness. Moses said "I can't speak!" Jeremiah said "I'm too young!" God qualifies the called. You don't need to be gifted. You need to be available.

Is this only for experienced Christians?

+

No! VOI is for anyone hungry to grow and multiply. We've had brand new believers and 30-year veterans. What matters is hunger, not history. If you want to impact lives, you're ready.

What happens after the 8 weeks?

+

You become a certified VOI facilitator. You continue discipling your 2-3 people. They disciple THEIR people. You stay connected through monthly VOI-wide gatherings. The multiplication never stops‚Äîit's a lifestyle, not a program.

Why only 25 people per cohort?

+

Quality over quantity. We want to know every person, track every growth, celebrate every breakthrough. Smaller cohorts allow for personalized support and genuine community. Once you multiply, your disciples will fill the next cohorts!
